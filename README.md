# Sales_dataset_SQL
This dataset records customer purchases across several product categories during the year 2023. It captures critical customer attributes like age and gender, alongside transaction details including product category, quantity purchased, and total amount spent. It is ideal for analyzing consumer behavior, identifying sales trends. 


# Table Name: Sales Dataset
# Columns:

	1) ID - This is unique id assigned to each transaction
	2) Date - Date of transaction
	3) Gender - Male or Female
	4) Age - Age of customer
	5) Product Category - Product which was purchased by customer
	6) Quantity - No of quantity purchased
	7) Price Per Unit - Price of 1 unit or 1 quantity
	8) Total Amount - sum of product (price per unit * quantity)
